Search tips
  • Perform a search using relevant keywords, title words or author names. If you have chosen "University Library OsloMet" in the dropdown menu, your search includes everything available in this library.
  • Use the search filters to the right of the results list to refine your results.
  • To expand your search to include books in other Norwegian academic libraries select "Norwegian academic libraries".
  • If you wish to access articles that are not available through OsloMet's library, tick the box marked "Include material your institution doesn't have access to".
